Admission Criteria

Bachelor's Program Requirements

  • High School Diploma or GED
  • Minimum GPA: 3.0 or higher
  • Standardized Test Scores: SAT or ACT required
  • English Proficiency: TOEFL/IELTS for international students
  • Letters of Recommendation: 2-3 academic references
  • Personal Statement: 500 words describing your goals
  • Application Fee: $50 (waived for early applicants)

Master's Program Requirements

  • Bachelor's Degree: In CS or related field
  • Minimum GPA: 3.2 from undergraduate program
  • GRE Scores: Quantitative score 150+
  • TOEFL/IELTS: Required for international students
  • Letters of Recommendation: 3 professional references
  • Statement of Purpose: Describing research interests
  • Resume/CV: Highlighting relevant experience
  • Application Fee: $75

Application Process

1

Prepare Documents

Gather transcripts, test scores, letters of recommendation, and personal statement.

2

Submit Application

Complete online application form at admissions.university.edu with all required documents.

3

Application Review

Our admissions committee reviews your application (2-4 weeks processing time).

4

Interview (Optional)

Qualified candidates may be invited for a virtual or in-person interview.

5

Admission Decision

Receive admission decision via email. Accept offer and complete enrollment.
